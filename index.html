About ThanniGo

Bridging the Gap Between
Local Shops & Modern Customers

Local businesses have always been the backbone of communities — but they've struggled to compete with the convenience of large e-commerce platforms. ThanniGo was built to change that imbalance.

We give shop owners powerful digital tools to manage their entire operations, customers the speed of instant local delivery with live tracking, and delivery partners a structured, reliable earning platform — all connected in real time on a single system.

The Problem

Local Commerce Was Fragmented.
We Built the Fix.

Every challenge in local commerce pointed to one gap: no unified platform. ThanniGo addresses each one head-on.

route

Fragmented Local Delivery

Customers had no reliable way to get deliveries from their nearby shops — no tracking, no coordination, no reliability.

storefront

Shops Without Digital Presence

Local shop owners operated entirely on pen-and-paper, missing digital customers and any form of inventory control.

pending_actions

Slow, Manual Order Management

Accepting, confirming, and dispatching orders required constant manual effort with high risk of errors and delays.

location_searching

Invisible Delivery Tracking

Once an order left the shop, customers had zero visibility — no live updates, no ETA, no communication channel.

forum

Communication Gaps

Coordinating between customers, shop staff, and delivery partners required endless phone calls and manual follow-ups.

payments

Cash-Only Operations

Without digital payment infrastructure, shops and customers were locked into cash transactions with no history or reconciliation.
